法则四：非同辈元素，任意一者及其祖元素不具备动态布局时，html靠后者居上。

情况1：两层嵌套

div1-parent {position:static}
div1 {position:static}
div2-parent {position:static}
div2 {position:static}

情况2：三层嵌套

div3-grand {position:static}
div3-parent {position:static}
div3 {position:static}
div4-grand {position:static}
div4-parent {position:static}
div4 {position:static}
